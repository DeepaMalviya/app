Services

MasterJi is custom crafted, bespoke tailoring application intended to offer quality sewed pieces of clothing, dress modifications, alterations, consultation and we stitch everything with guaranteed bother free fitting at moderate costs with free get and conveyance administrations.

Stitching

We stitch almost everything whether it is blouse, lehenga, shirt, pantaloons, suits, blazers regardless of both men, women & children..

Mending

We also do offer mending services for clothes but with an order of five clothes at least in one time.


Customisation

We serve a wide range of designs to our clients to choose from whichever suits to their personality specifically for women, we deliver as per their wish and requirements..

Consultation

MasterJi do serve consultation service to our customers who are willing to get assistance for perfect fit cloth selection from our wide range of designs and custom tailoring service.
